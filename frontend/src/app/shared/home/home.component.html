Going to the dentist every now and then is an important part of maintaining good oral health. It's easy to forget about going to the dentist when everything seems fine, but regular check-ups can help catch any issues early on and prevent them from becoming bigger problems down the line. Plus, getting your teeth cleaned professionally can help remove plaque and tartar that you may have missed while brushing and flossing at home. So don't forget to schedule your regular dental appointments - your teeth (and wallet) will thank you in the long run!
